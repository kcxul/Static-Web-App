Join Our Team

Joining our team at Fotos De Film means becoming part of a passionate and dynamic community of creative individuals who are dedicated to producing exceptional photography. As a member of our team, you'll have the opportunity to work with cutting-edge equipment, collaborate with talented professionals, and unleash your creativity in a supportive environment. We value innovation, teamwork, and continuous growth, and we are committed to fostering a workplace where your skills are recognized and your ideas are valued. If you're looking to be part of a team that thrives on pushing boundaries, delivering excellence, and capturing the beauty of life through the lens, we invite you to explore our current job openings and join us on our exciting journey.

Lighting Technician

As a Lighting Technician at Fotos De Film, you will play a crucial role in ensuring that our photography and videography projects are executed with the highest level of professionalism and creativity. Your responsibilities will include setting up and adjusting lighting equipment, collaborating with photographers and videographers to achieve the desired lighting effects, and maintaining a keen eye for detail to enhance the visual appeal of our projects. The ideal candidate will have a strong understanding of various lighting techniques, excellent problem-solving skills, and a passion for creating visually stunning content.

Photographer

As a Photographer at Fotos De Film, you will have the opportunity to showcase your creativity and technical expertise in capturing memorable moments and producing exceptional visual content. Whether it's shooting events, portraits, or specialized photography projects, you will be responsible for using your artistic vision and technical skills to deliver outstanding results. We are looking for individuals with a strong portfolio, a keen eye for composition and lighting, and the ability to work effectively in a team environment.

Videographer

Join our team as a Videographer and be part of creating captivating visual stories through videography. As a Videographer at Fotos De Film, you will be responsible for capturing and editing video content that meets the highest standards of quality and creativity. From filming events to producing promotional videos, you will have the opportunity to bring your unique perspective to our projects. The ideal candidate will possess strong technical skills in video production, proficiency in video editing software, and a passion for storytelling through visual media.
